The Mission

Golf was never supposed to be for them. We're taking it back.

Fairplay Studios exists to dismantle the gatekeeping that has kept golf a closed club for too long. We're not fighting the game. We're fighting the culture around it. The dress codes, the membership walls, the silent rules that say: this isn't for you.
